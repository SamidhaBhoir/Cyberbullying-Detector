Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our application that integrates with Instagram via the Meta Graph API.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information through Instagram APIs:

  • Instagram account ID
  • Username and profile information
  • Media data (posts, captions, comments)
  • Engagement data such as comments and follower count

2. How We Use Information

The collected data is used strictly for:

  • Displaying Instagram content
  • Managing comments (replying, hiding, moderating)
  • Analyzing engagement metrics

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only used within the application.

4. Data Storage

We do not permanently store personal data. Any data accessed via the API is used temporarily and handled securely.

5. Permissions

Our application requests permissions such as:

  • instagram_basic
  • instagram_manage_comments
  • instagram_content_publish

These permissions are used solely to provide core application functionality.

6. User Control

You can revoke access to our application at any time through your Facebook/Instagram settings.

7. Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
